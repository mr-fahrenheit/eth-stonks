how to play


to play eth stonks, you'll need to:
1) install  or another ethereum wallet
2) buy some ETH from an exchange
3) withdraw the ETH to your new wallet
4) get some USDT from Uniswap

once you have some USDT:
1) register a username
2) buy companies to start producing stonks
3) sell or invest your stonks

stonks

- companies can be bought for USDT
- each company produces 1 stonk per second
- companies cannot be sold
- stonks can be sold for USDT
- stonks can be invested into new companies

stonkbrokers


buy with a stonkbroker to get 5% cashback!

to use a stonkbroker you must visit their link, e.g:
https://ethstonks.finance/#/MrF

to become a stonkbroker you must spend $1000+ during a round or be granted special access

stonkbrokers receive 5% from referred buys and 10% cashback for their own buys, including the first buy that qualifies

market behavior

buying increases stonk value

funds added + minor inflation

reinvesting preserves stonk value

funds unchanged + no inflation

sells decrease stonk value

funds removed + major inflation

round mechanics

the market can hit up to 3 circuit breakers
each circuit breaker awards 7 bailouts:

- biggest spender
- biggest producer
- last 5 buys

however, if the market crash timer runs out then the bailout fund goes to the stonk fund and the round is over
